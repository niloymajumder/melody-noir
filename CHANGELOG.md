# Changelog

All notable changes to Melody Noir theme will be documented in this file.

## [v2.0.0] — Gorillaz x Melody Noir

First three themes from the Gorillaz album *The Mountain* (2025) — more songs coming in next updates:

- **Orange County** — Warm sunset California melancholy. Burnt orange, golden amber, and dusty rose over a deep warm background.
- **The Mountain** — Epic mountain ascent at misty dawn. Mountain sage, rocky amber, and sky slate blue over cool dark stone.
- **The Sad God** — Sacred cave bioluminescence. Moonlit blues, ritual fire gold, and mystic lavender over a deep blue-black void.

### Changes

- Added three Gorillaz Edition themes under `Melody Noir: Gorillaz Editions`
- Completely redesigned The Sad God palette for deeper cave atmosphere
- Full 267-key UI color coverage + 73+ tokenColor scopes per theme
- Terminal and Git integration for all three themes
